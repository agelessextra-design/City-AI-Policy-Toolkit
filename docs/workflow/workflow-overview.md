# Workflow Overview

## Core Sequence

1. readiness and module-family selection
2. context discovery and local input collection
3. source audit and neutralization when source-backed material is involved
4. template selection
5. outline building
6. draft generation
7. evaluation
8. review sprint preparation
9. human review and targeted signoff
10. packaging and maintenance

## Module-Family Logic

The toolkit is organized around five connected module families:

- strategic leadership
- governance and policy
- workforce and learning
- operations and service delivery
- community trust

They should be treated as connected parts of one city system, not isolated workstreams.

## Dependency Pattern

- strategic leadership sets priorities, sequencing, and scale-up conditions
- governance defines controls, approval paths, accountability, and escalation
- workforce defines staff readiness, manager support, and labor touchpoints
- operations defines intake, delivery, support, monitoring, and retirement mechanics
- community trust defines public explanation, accessibility, feedback, and redress

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- do not skip from intake straight to prose
- do not invent local authority structures
- do not hide unresolved decisions to make a document read smoothly
- do not treat evaluation as optional
- do not use examples as default policy text
