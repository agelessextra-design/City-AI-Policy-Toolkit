# Prompt Pack: Operations Service Delivery Hardening

## Purpose

This prompt pack is used after the first operations build pass to repair issues, resolve contradictions, lift low scores, and prepare operational artifacts for board-style review.

## Shared Inputs

- `toolkit/prompts/operations-and-service-delivery/prompt-pack-operations-service-delivery.md`
- `toolkit/evaluations/guide-module-family-review-readiness.md`
- `toolkit/evaluations/template-module-family-scorecard.md`
- `toolkit/evaluations/template-shared-issue-log.md`
- `toolkit/review-sprint-kit/template-contradiction-sweep-checklist.md`
- relevant operations templates and adapter notes
- current issue log and contradiction sweep results

## Pack 5: Operations Repair

Return:

1. issue-by-issue repair plan
2. exact sections to revise
3. unresolved launch, support, or rollback decisions
4. revised handoff target

Rules:

- do not hide weak operational support or pause authority gaps
- keep governance, workforce, and trust dependencies explicit

## Pack 6: Operations Contradiction Resolution

Use when operational playbooks conflict with governance approvals, workforce assumptions, or resident-support commitments.

Return:

1. contradiction inventory
2. preferred resolution path
3. impacted artifacts
4. required owner decisions

## Pack 7: Operations Upgrade

Use when operational assets are usable but below target.

Return:

1. score-lift plan by dimension
2. highest-leverage rewrites
3. missing evidence or companion artifacts
4. next evaluation checkpoint

## Pack 8: Operations Board Prep

Return:

1. likely board objections
2. proof points already present
3. weak spots needing hardening
4. review packet additions needed before board simulation
